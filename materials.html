Materials

Material systems that make concept objects believable.

A high-end art furniture brand must show how visual language becomes matter: metal, mineral texture, wood, glass, light, surface treatment, and digital records.

材料页面的作用是降低客户的不确定感：不是只有图，而是知道可以怎么做出来。

01

Metal

Stainless steel, aluminum, brass-like finishes, blackened metal, polished or brushed surfaces, and concealed structural details.

02

Mineral Surface

Stone-like textures, mineral coating, composite surfaces, and soft sculptural masses for heavier spatial presence.

03

Wood + Structure

Internal frames, wood base systems, hidden reinforcement, and controlled weight for transport and installation.

04

Glass

Display cabinets, protective surfaces, reflections, transparent volumes, and controlled visual depth.

05

Light

Embedded glow, low-profile fixtures, edge lighting, indirect ambience, and light as part of the object identity.

06

Digital Archive

Every commission can keep a digital record: concept, model, material options, production notes, and final object identity.

Material Decision

Material is selected by use, not only appearance.

Indoor humidity, outdoor exposure, body contact, cleaning frequency, lighting, installation route, and budget all change the correct material choice.
