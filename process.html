Process

A commission route from space to object.

The process must make high-end clients feel safe: clear stages, clear decisions, and clear points where cost, scale, and feasibility are checked.

这是把“艺术想法”变成“可以执行的生意”的关键页面。

01

Space Reading

Collect room photos, plan drawings, function, light, traffic flow, and desired atmosphere.

02

Object Direction

Choose the object family: bench, table, cabinet, light, or a mixed spatial object.

03

Concept Proposal

Present shape direction, material route, scale range, budget logic, and production risk.

04

Prototype / 3D Study

Build proportion studies, renders, structural notes, and details for client approval.

05

Fabrication

Coordinate fabrication, surface finish, hidden structure, samples, and quality checks.

06

Delivery

Plan packing, transport, installation, maintenance notes, and digital object archive.

Typical timeline

Concept1–2 weeks depending on references and site clarity.
Prototype study2–4 weeks depending on scale and material complexity.
Fabrication4–12 weeks depending on object size, finish, supplier schedule, and installation conditions.

Client should prepare

  • Project location and space type
  • Photos, drawings, dimensions, and target use
  • Preferred object family and material direction
  • Budget range and desired timeline
  • Designer or project manager contact if applicable
