Atelier

A place for form, craft, and spatial memory.

Archive Objects is not positioned as a small design studio. It is an art-furniture atelier: a place where sculptural form, material research, spatial reading, and project delivery are brought into one controlled process.

Atelier 的意思不是普通“工作室”，而是创作、工艺、材料和落地能力统一发生的造物工坊。它更适合艺术家具与高端定制。

Maison Logic

A brand world, not a catalogue seller.

The atelier supports the larger Maison idea: Archive Objects builds a world of usable art objects for private spaces, cultural rooms, boutique hotels, clubs, galleries, and designer-led projects.

01

Spatial Reading

Every object begins by reading the room: entrance rhythm, light, body movement, visual pressure, and where a memory point should exist.

02

Form Language

Sculptural language is translated into usable proportions, touchable surfaces, and objects that can live inside real interiors.

03

Realization

Material, structure, transport, installation, and long-term maintenance stay visible from the concept stage.

Savoir-faire

The craft language behind the objects.

The aim is not to make decoration. The aim is to create usable objects with presence: furniture that carries the weight of sculpture and the discipline of craft.
