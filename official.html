Brand Position

Between furniture, sculpture, and spatial memory.

Each object is designed to be used, lived with, and remembered. The work begins from a space rather than a catalogue: light, movement, material, emotional tone, and the people who will experience it.

01

Usable

Objects must serve real spatial functions: seating, surface, storage, light, atmosphere, and circulation.

02

Sculptural

Every piece carries a formal identity strong enough to become a memory point in a room.

03

Commissioned

Pieces are adapted to villas, hotels, clubs, galleries, and designer-led interiors rather than mass produced.
